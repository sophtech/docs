Soph lets you:

* **Design**:  We will use AI to design the fashionable outfits you need, you only need a description or parameters to complete the task.

* **Clothes**: Depending on your design you will be shown 2D or 3D clothes, if not satisfied you will be able to redesign.

* **Supply Chain**: According to our supply chain system, we can take your design, provide it to the factory for production and send it to you.

* **Tariff**: If there is a factory in your country that produces it, there are no customs duties to pay, otherwise you will need to pay them.
